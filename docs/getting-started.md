## Demo Notebook

The [`examples/simpl_demo.ipynb`](https://github.com/TomGeorge1234/SIMPL/blob/main/examples/simpl_demo.ipynb) notebook walks through the full SIMPL workflow:

1. **Synthetic grid cells** — fits SIMPL on artificial grid cell data with known ground truth.
2. **Real place cells** — fits SIMPL on real hippocampal place cell recordings from [Tanni et al. (2022)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35835121/).
